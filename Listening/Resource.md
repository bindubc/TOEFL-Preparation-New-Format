Here are some valuable resources and suggestions to help you practice and improve your listening skills for the TOEFL:

### 1. **Official TOEFL Resources**
   - **TOEFL Official Website**: The official TOEFL website provides sample questions, practice tests, and information on the listening section.
     - [TOEFL Official Website](https://www.ets.org/toefl)
   - **TOEFL Practice Tests**: ETS offers official practice tests and materials for a more authentic experience.
     - [Official TOEFL Practice Tests](https://www.ets.org/toefl/test-takers/ibt/prepare)

### 2. **TOEFL Listening Practice**
   - **Testden**: Offers free TOEFL listening practice tests to simulate the actual exam.
     - [Testden TOEFL Listening Practice](https://www.testden.com/toefl/listening/)
   - **Magoosh TOEFL Blog**: Magoosh provides great tips and free resources to improve listening skills, including sample questions.
     - [Magoosh TOEFL Listening Tips](https://magoosh.com/toefl/toefl-listening-strategy/)
   - **EdX (TOEFL Preparation Course)**: Offers free and paid TOEFL prep courses that include listening practice.
     - [EdX TOEFL Preparation](https://www.edx.org/course/toefl-test-preparation)

### 3. **Listening to Lectures & Talks**
   - **TED Talks**: Listening to TED talks will expose you to diverse academic topics and formal speech. You can turn on subtitles to better follow the content and then practice listening without them.
     - [TED Talks](https://www.ted.com/talks)
   - **BBC Learning English**: Offers a variety of listening exercises, including academic-based content.
     - [BBC Learning English](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish)
   - **YouTube Educational Channels**: Channels like “CrashCourse” and “Khan Academy” feature educational videos with clear, structured lectures on various topics.
     - [CrashCourse on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/user/crashcourse)
     - [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org)

### 4. **Podcasts for Academic Listening**
   - **The English We Speak (BBC)**: Short episodes focusing on real-world English usage, perfect for improving academic listening.
     - [The English We Speak](https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/p02pc9xk/episodes/downloads)
   - **Academic English Listening Podcast**: Focused on improving academic listening skills, it features short lectures on different subjects.
     - [Academic English Listening](https://www.listeningpractice.org/academic-podcast/)

### 5. **Listening Practice Apps**
   - **TOEFL Listening Practice**: This app offers listening practice tests, timed exercises, and question breakdowns.
     - Available on both [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.practiceenglish) and [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/toefl-listening-practice/id1283367457).
   - **ELSA Speak**: An app that helps improve your pronunciation and listening by simulating conversations in academic contexts.
     - [ELSA Speak](https://www.elsaspeak.com/)

### 6. **Additional Resources for Building Focus and Stamina**
   - **Mindfulness Apps (Headspace or Calm)**: Practice mindfulness techniques to improve focus and concentration, which will benefit your listening during the exam.
     - [Headspace](https://www.headspace.com)
     - [Calm](https://www.calm.com)

### 7. **Vocabulary and Concept Building**
   - **Quizlet**: Use Quizlet to create or find TOEFL-specific flashcards to help with vocabulary building and listening comprehension.
     - [Quizlet TOEFL Vocabulary](https://quizlet.com)
   - **Anki**: A spaced repetition app where you can find or create decks for TOEFL-related academic terms and phrases.
     - [Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net)

### 8. **Listening Strategies**
   - **Improving Listening Skills with Steve Kaufmann**: Learn from a language expert on how to improve listening comprehension over time.
     - [Steve Kaufmann's YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC-2vWLNwr9gk36Y7b98gk0A)
   - **Listening Comprehension Strategies by TOEFL iBT**: A resource that teaches effective strategies for answering listening questions.
     - [Listening Comprehension Strategies](https://www.toefltestpro.com/listening-strategy/)

### 9. **Listening Exercises for Long Lectures**
   - **Listenwise**: An educational podcast platform that provides listening exercises and activities based on real-world topics, perfect for building stamina for long lectures.
     - [Listenwise](https://listenwise.com)

By incorporating a variety of these resources into your daily routine, you'll be able to improve your listening skills, build stamina, and feel more confident during the TOEFL listening section.
